Your Pig Depends On You

Protect the Piggy requires you to defend your most important asset during the zombie apocalypse, your piggy. Protect your piggy from the incoming waves of zombies for as long as you can. Defend against zombies of all speeds, shapes, and sizes. How long can you keep your piggy safe?

Update Your Loudout

Stronger zombies will require stronger weapons to defeat them. But better knives and guns in the shop that increase your damage and attack speed. Some weapons even have special abilities to help you defeat more zombies.

Customize Your Look

Go to the costumes menu and customize your farmer however you wish. All costumes are available to you from the start of the game. If you can get one of the top 5 scores in the world, your piggy skin will be displayed on the leaderboard as well.
