Quad Qannon

Quad Qannon was created in the Unity game engine as part of Game Jam Q, hosted by the Stevens Game Development Club at Stevens Institute of Technology. The jam was 48 hours long and had the theme "quad". Quad Qannon is a four-player game that uses an unorthodox control scheme. Each player gets one four-key corner of the keyboard for turning left, turning right, select the next cannon, and repair cannon. After 10 seconds, every cannon fires in the direction that it is currently facing. A cannon that is hit by a bullet is destroyed and must be repaired by the player. The last player standing wins.
