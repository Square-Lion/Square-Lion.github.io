Just Another Day At The Coffee Shop

To The Bunker! is a game where you are the personal driver of the President of the United States. One day while the President is at their daily visit to their favorite coffee shop, the streets are suddenly filled with assassins. You must get the President from the coffee shop to the bunker on the other side of town.

Multiple Paths To Choose From

While the bunker is straight down the road in front of you, more assassins clog the main road than the smaller side roads. Analyze the map before selecting a level to pick the best path for you to take.
